# usage of vi/vim under windows:

## move line

* up --> k

* down -->j

* left -->h

* right -->l

* linehead-->0

* noneBlankLineHead--> shift+^

* linetail-->shift+$

* up one line --> ctrl +e

* down one line --> ctrl +y

* to N line ---> :n    or-->nG

----

## move word

* word before  start--->w

* 				end --->e

* word after after -->b

* 				end --> ge

* to somechar  left-->Tx

* 				right--->fx

---
